Expedia Customer Service

Need help with your Expedia booking? You're in the right place. Here's a full 2025 guide on how to reach Expedia's customer support quickly and easily.

1. Expedia Customer Service Phone Number

Call +1-888-829-1117 to speak directly with Expedia's 24/7 support team for bookings, changes, or cancellations.

2. Use Expedia Live Chat

Visit the official Expedia website and initiate a live chat with a support agent in real-time.

3. Email Expedia

You can send a detailed query via their contact email: support@Expedia.com (subject to change).

4. Check the Help Center

Explore articles and FAQs in the Expedia Help Center for instant answers.

5. Ask Through the App

Use the Expedia mobile app to access your booking and contact support directly from your phone.

6. Message on Social Media

Reach out to Expedia via Twitter @Expedia or Facebook for quick responses.

7. Contact via Form

Use the contact form on Expedia's website to explain your issue and receive a follow-up.

8. Get Help On Existing Bookings

Log in to your Expedia account, go to “My Trips,” select your booking and click on “Manage” to request help or changes.

9. Request a Callback

Some support options allow you to schedule a callback instead of waiting on hold.

10. Travel Agent Support

If booked via agent, they can contact Expedia on your behalf using priority support channels.
